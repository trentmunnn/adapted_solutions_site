Digital Visibility
What is AEO and GEO optimization?

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) are techniques to ensure your website is correctly identified and recommended by AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. Unlike traditional SEO which targets Google's ranking algorithm, AEO/GEO specifically targets how AI systems understand and recommend your business.

Why does it matter if AI recommends my business?

Research shows customers are 8x more likely to use a business that's been recommended by an AI assistant. As more people use ChatGPT, voice search, and Google AI Overviews to find services, businesses that aren't visible to these systems are losing customers to competitors who are.

What does the free audit check?

Our free 16-point audit checks four categories: structured data (schema markup, JSON-LD), GEO signals (llms.txt, Open Graph tags, entity clarity), content structure (headings, FAQ sections, meta descriptions), and technical baseline (HTTPS, canonical tags, sitemap). You get scored in each category and your top issues are called out immediately — no signup required.

What is included in the Full Site Reimagination?

The Site Reimagination is our premium Digital Visibility offering — a complete rebuild of your web presence on our AI-enabled architecture. It includes a new site with built-in AEO/GEO optimization, an AI-powered CMS you can update through conversation, blog automation, content seeding across Reddit/LinkedIn/Google Business, and monthly next best action recommendations to continuously grow your digital footprint.
