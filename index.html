AI and chatbot solutions from Adapted Solutions can handle customer inquiries, manage work orders, and surface business insights 24/7 without adding headcount. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) are techniques to ensure your website is correctly identified and recommended by AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.
